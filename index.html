Developer Reference

GET /api/route-safety-score

Retrieves safety metrics for a specific maritime route. Use route_id=HOR-MAL for the Strait of Hormuz to Malacca route.

GET /api/energy-tax-logic

Calculates comprehensive taxes and storage fees. Parameters: country, quantity (m3), months, value (USD).

GET /api/port-infrastructure-specs

Fetches technical infrastructure data for major ports. ?port=ULSAN or ?port=FUJAIRAH.

GET /api/market-prices

Returns the latest daily energy price benchmarks (Brent, WTI, Gasoline, Diesel) synced from EIA and Opinet.
